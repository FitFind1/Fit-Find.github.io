About Fit Find

Have you ever ordered something online and it ended up being too big? Or too small? Or the fabric was not like advertised? Fit Find is here to fix that! With Fit Find, shopping has never been easier, and you get the exact product you were imagining! Simply download Fit Find, insert your measurements and voila - you're ready to get the best online shopping experience you could ask for!

How it Works

Getting Started

Step 1: Install the FitFind app on mobile or download the chrome extension on your computer
Step 2: Create an account and begin creating your avatar!
Step 3: Explore the many features that FitFind offers that will change your perception of online shopping

Features

-Visual representation of an item on your avatar

-Suggestions on the best size for an individual depending on measurements and preferred fit

-Use of Augmented reality to see the real life size/measurements on the customer

-Quality reviews for clothes with point system incentive

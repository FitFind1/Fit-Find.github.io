About Fit Find

Have you ever ordered something online, and it ended up being too big? Or too small? Or the fabric was not like advertised? Fit Find is here to fix that! With Fit Find, shopping has never been easier, and you get the exact product you were imagining! Simply download Fit Find, insert your measurements and voilà ‒ you’re ready to get the best online shopping experience you could ask for!

How it Works

We have various features on the Fit Find app to help you achieve the best shopping experience possible. Some of our features include: a visual representation of an item's fit using augmented reality, customization of an avatar with different brands of clothes, and a point system for reviewers. Fit Find also suggests the best products for you based on your measurements; preferred fit; and most importantly, reviews. In combination, these features work together to ensure you the best possible shopping experience that you cannot find anywhere else!
